## Class Roles & Recommendations

### Pure Tanks (Heavy Armor, High HP, Physical Defense)
- **DarkKnight** - Highest HP (482), excellent physical defense
- **Warden** - High HP (464), best Heavy Melee AP (90)
- **Paladin** - Hybrid tank/healer, good all-around

### DPS Warriors (Heavy Armor, High Damage)
- **Dragoon** - Highest melee damage (94 AP), burst specialist
- **Duelist** - High evasion stats, counter-attack style
- **DarkKnight** - Can sacrifice HP for damage

### Agile DPS (Light Armor, Fast Attacks)
- **Ranger** - Best physical DPS (94 AP), high range
- **Gunslinger** - High burst, critical specialist
- **Monk** - Combo system, sustained damage
- **Rogue** - Stealth, sneak attack burst

### Hybrid Specialist (Unique)
- **Alchemist** - Ranged Hybrid, AoE specialist, utility

### Pure Casters (Cloth Armor, High Magic Damage)
- **Wizard** - Highest damage (110 AP), AoE control
- **Sorcerer** - High damage (102 AP), spell modification
- **Druid** - Versatile, healing + damage

### Support/Healers (Cloth Armor, High MP)
- **Cleric** - Primary healer (102 AP, 594 MP)
- **Bard** - Buffer (640 MP, party-wide buffs)
- **Necromancer** - Debuffer/DoTs
